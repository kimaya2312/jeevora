  content: "Jeevora is a privacy-first UK health app that helps patients track symptoms, understand their health, and prepare for GP appointments — without interfering with NHS systems. NHS England's waiting list stands at around 7.11 million, and the average GP appointment lasts just 10 minutes. When symptoms overlap — fatigue, mood shifts, medication side effects, poor sleep — it becomes hard to know what is connected and harder still to explain it clearly in the time you have. Jeevora helps you log, separate, and make sense of it all before you walk in."

      - "Captures appointment concerns and questions to raise with your GP"
      - "Generates a clear, non-diagnostic GP-ready report"
      - "Tracks sleep, mood, meals, exercise, and lifestyle changes"
      - "Designed to connect with major wearable devices"
